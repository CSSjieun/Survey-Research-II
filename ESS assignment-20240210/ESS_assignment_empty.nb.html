Assignment

As a member of a consulting firm crafting a climate change campaign, understanding public opinion on various public policies is crucial for its success. Specifically, insights into generational attitudes towards fossil fuels and renewable energy policies are essential. Conduct an analysis using data from the European Social Survey on climate change, focusing on the 2016-2017 data (Round 8), to inform the campaigns direction.

  • Download R8 data for all availabel countries
  • Plot the proportion of people who think that worlds climate is definitely changing (clmchng) by country
  • Employ bivariate models to analyze the relationship between age groups and their support for this specific policies aimed at mitigating climate change.
  1. Increasing taxes on fossil fuels, such as oil, gas and coal, inctxff
  2. Using public money to subsidise renewable energy such as wind and solar power,sbsrnen
  • Make a diagnosis

The variable age group is categorized as follows:

  • >25
  • 25-44
  • 45-64
  • >=65
